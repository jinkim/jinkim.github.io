Our Leadership & Expertise

Our strength comes from our people—experienced industry professionals, engineers, and problem solvers. Our team’s deep expertise, combined with institutional knowledge built over decades, allows us to continually innovate and lead in materials recovery and industrial sustainability.




Bill Sulak

President, Ferrous Processing & Trading

Not Preview Mode

Bill Sulak is the President of Ferrous Processing & Trading. Mr. Sulak is responsible for overseeing and directing all operations, commercial activity, business development and growth strategy for FPT. Mr. Sulak has over 18 years of operations and leadership experience in metal recycling. He started his career with FPT in 2007 as an Account Executive in Detroit prior to becoming a Senior Sales Manager at FPT’s Ohio location in 2012. In 2014, he was promoted to Regional Director for the Southern region and was appointed to the Executive Leadership Team in 2015. Prior to his position as President, he was the Executive Vice President of the Southern and Mexico regions. In addition, Mr. Sulak sits on several boards and committees, including the Recycled Materials Association (ReMA), Gemini Recycling, and New Copper Country, and has recently served as the President of the Southeast region of ReMA.

As a leader, Mr. Sulak strongly believes in servant leadership and prioritizes the growth and well-being of the team. He attributes his team’s passion and grit to FPT’s innovation and success in the industry. In addition, Mr. Sulak has a bachelor’s degree from the University of Michigan – Dearborn.


Drew Luntz

President, Ferrous Processing & Trading Ohio

Not Preview Mode

Andrew (Drew) Luntz is the Regional President of Ferrous Processing & Trading Ohio. Mr. Luntz is responsible for efficiently directing all activity of FPT’s Northeast Ohio facilities. He started in the industry in 1977 as an administrative assistant at Luntz Corporation and moved throughout the company in various roles, including Plant Manager, Vice President of Operations, Chief Operating Officer and President, learning all phases of the business and developing essential skills that serve FPT today. As a fourth-generation member of the Luntz scrap metal family, Mr. Luntz brings decades of generational metal recycling knowledge and shredding know-how to FPT.

As a leader, Mr. Luntz looks forward to how the advancement of technology will progressively change the way metal is recycled and takes pride in recycling and the resources it provides to the industry. Previously, Mr. Luntz was the President of Phillips Metals. In addition, he has a bachelor’s degree in economics from Hiram College.

Mario Macari

Executive Vice President of Operations

Not Preview Mode

Mario Macari is the Executive Vice President of Operations of Ferrous Processing & Trading. Mr. Macari is responsible for all aspects of operations at FPT facilities, including management, planning, and process improvement. His extensive background in metal recycling, logistics management, and demolition spans over 35 years and has been vital in successfully transforming multiple FPT operations. Since joining the company, Mr. Macari has managed operations at every FPT yard in Michigan as well as the company’s Cleveland, Miami, and Ontario locations. Mr. Macari has learned what it takes to successfully manage FPT operations from the ground up, having begun his metal recycling career as a scale operator and dispatcher in 1990 at Schlafer Iron & Steel prior to becoming their Operations Manager.

As a leader, Mr. Macari thrives on knowing that every day provides another opportunity to innovate and stay ahead of the competition. Mr. Macari has a dual degree in hotel restaurant management and culinary arts at Henry Ford Community College.

Tony Benacquisto

Senior Executive Advisor to the President

Not Preview Mode

Anthony (Tony) Benacquisto is the Senior Executive Advisor to the President of Ferrous Processing & Trading. Mr. Benaquisto is responsible for advising on all yard operations, capital programs, and other growth initiatives as directed by the President. Having touched every aspect of the industry, Mr. Benacquisto brings decades of heavy equipment expertise and operations experience to FPT. Previously, Mr. Benacquisto served as Executive Vice President of Operations to rebuild all FPT shredding and baling operations, downstream and separation systems, and sand flow systems. He was also instrumental in building zero-emission shredding systems at FPT. Since 1976, Mr. Benacquisto was an Operations Manager, then Vice President of Operations at Schlafer Iron & Steel before acquiring the company with his partners. They later purchased Sam Allen & Sons, forming TBS Recycling, one of the largest privately held scrap processors in Michigan. Mr. Benacquisto joined FPT through acquisition in 1997.

As a leader, Mr. Benacquisto believes that the best way to learn the business is from the bottom up with an entrepreneurial spirit in order to gain a true understanding of every facet of the business. Furthermore, he enjoys working collaboratively with his team to build new systems and new ways to operate while always keeping safety at the forefront. Mr. Benacquisto has a degree in education from Northern Michigan University and has also served in the U.S. Navy from 1968 to 1971.
