SOLUTIONS

Advanced Processing & Material Recovery



01

Ferrous & Non-Ferrous Metal Recycling

FPT is a premier processor and broker of ferrous and non-ferrous metals, ensuring these materials are refined and reintegrated into manufacturing with maximum efficiency.


Capabilities

Processing capabilities include full-service yards with shredders, high-speed balers and custom product configurations for downstream manufacturing.

Volume

FPT handles over 3 million tons of recycled metal annually, supplying premium ferrous and non-ferrous products to mills, foundries and consumers around the world.

Quality

Measured, tested and proven. Our dedicated Quality Department combines chemical analysis, in-house testing and continuous process improvement to ensure every shipment meets or exceeds customer expectations. Across all facilities, we utilize handheld analyzers and rigorous quality assurance protocols to deliver consistently high-grade materials to ferrous and non-ferrous consumers worldwide.

In addition to having our own quality department and testing, FPT will utilize reputable external 3rd party raw material and/or metallurgical laboratory services

02

Materials Separation & Processing

Utilizing optical separation systems and cutting-edge processing technologies to extract maximum value from materials.


Advanced Systems

FPT’s technologically advanced media plants, sortation lines and drainage systems are designed to recover valuable materials with minimal environmental impact.

Reduced Landfill Materials

Our investments in sortation technology not only increase purity and resale value but also reduce landfill materials.

Foundry Products

Specialty products—developed through innovation in processing techniques:

  • uncoated black punching and busheling 1006/08/10
  • prime shred
  • low-copper shred
  • shredded #1 clips
  • gray and nodular iron
  • hot-process briquettes
  • dense free-flowing nuggets of low-residual carbon scrap
  • coolant – 4 and under – can be washed/dried and packaged if required
  • slitter
Custom Material Blends

Our ability to create customer-specific material blends sets us apart in both domestic and international markets.

Operational Efficiency



05

Reduced Downtime Material Management

From discarded metal removal to transportation and on-site logistics, we design systems that keep our customers’ operations moving without interruption. Our infrastructure, equipment and accountability measures ensure consistent performance and reduce inefficiencies across the entire process.


Turnkey In-Plant Programs

FPT’s turnkey industrial metal scrap management programs include in-plant material handling, segregation strategy, resale optimization and logistics—customized to your facility.

Equipment Provisioning

Roll-off containers, hoppers and custom on-site equipment are provided to match your operational needs.

24/7 Service

Around-the-clock removal services help you avoid material buildup and costly downtime.

Maximizing Resale Value

Our marketing experts and process engineers work closely with manufacturers to maximize discarded metal value through expert sorting, handling and alignment with market demand—all while minimizing operational friction.

06

Logistics & Transportation Infrastructure

Managing a private fleet of over 1,400 railcars and trucking assets to optimize discarded metal movement, reduce costs and enhance supply chain efficiency.


Integrated Logistics & Equipment Support

FPT operates a full-service logistics program with 24/7 dispatch and monitoring, seamlessly coordinating transportation and equipment services to support everything from retail pickups to high-volume industrial operations.

Global Reach

We move materials efficiently around the world with optimized routes and a strong logistics network.

Extensive Railcar Fleet

Our fleet of over 1,400 railcars supports high-volume material movement across long distances.

07

Accuracy & Accountability

Every ounce of material that moves through our system is tracked, validated and documented. From certified scales and real-time weight verification to video monitoring and radiation screening, FPT ensures complete transparency and confidence at every step of the process—for our customers.


Certified Scales

All facility scales are state certified and calibrated regularly under our ISO 9001 quality program.

Real-Time Weight Verification

Each load is electronically recorded and weight tickets are available within minutes via FPT’s Client Portal.

Video Load Monitoring

Loads are photographed, video recorded, and timestamped so we can ensure accuracy by validating load weights upon arrival to ensure accurate grading and uphold transparency.

Visual Inspections & Radiation Screening

Every load is visually inspected, with digital photo documentation and advanced radiation detection systems in place.
